美国PCE计算器

中文: 美国心脏病学会（ACC）和美国心脏协会（AHA）发布的Pooled Cohort Equations（PCE）计算器可以帮助评估10年内发生心血管疾病的风险。该计算器适用于20-79岁的无心血管病史的成年人。

English: The American College of Cardiology (ACC) and the American Heart Association (AHA) have developed the Pooled Cohort Equations (PCE) calculator to assess the 10-year risk of cardiovascular disease events. This tool is suitable for adults aged 20-79 years who have no history of cardiovascular disease.
